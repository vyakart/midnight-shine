My work is to be a quiet bridge — helping the right people meet, share, care and build together. If our connection has helped/benefitted you in some way, I'd appreciate your support.

I've setup a number that covers my essential living expenses. This allows me to dedicate time fully to connecting people, exploring ideas and building communities. The number is adjusted annually, but I don’t expect it to rise beyond this amount for at least the next two years.

Where Support Goes

Where your support goes

  • Health & wellbeing – 25% (insurance, nutrition, fitness)
  • Subscriptions & learning – 20% (AI tools, courses, research material)
  • Personal expenses – 30% (rent, food, essentials)
  • Work travel – 15% (meeting collaborators, field visits)
  • Savings & buffer – 10% (unexpected costs, reinvestment)

If support goes beyond my annual goal

Any excess amount will not be kept for myself. Instead, it will be directed into a pool of charities I personally recommend and to individuals whose work I trust and endorse. In this way, your support ripples outward.

Why not fiat payments?

I registered a payment gateway but found that it required policies that infringed on privacy. Until I find a better way to safeguard identities, I’m currently using a Buy Me a Coffee page for contributions.

If that doesn’t work for you, simply send me an email — we’ll figure out a way that works for both of us.
